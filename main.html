How It Works

Our system collects feedback from students about their local tourism experiences and school field trips. This data helps educators and tourism operators improve future experiences.

  1. Before the Visit

    Fill out your personal info, choose your prefered locations, who your are going with, and what you expect from the trips.

  2. Experience the Site

    Visit the chosen Manila tourist spots as part of your school field experiences and local tourism activities.

  3. After the Visit

    Rate your experience - cleanliness, staff interaction, pricing, crowd management and share what you learned.
